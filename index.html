How to Become a Partner?

Are you interested in becoming a partner with us? Here’s how you can start the process of becoming a partner:

1. Application on Our Discord

To become a partner, you need to apply on our Discord server. The application process ensures that our partners are a good fit for our community and that both sides benefit.

2. Benefits of Partnership

After a successful application and acceptance as a partner, you will have the opportunity to showcase your server on our website and in our Discord server. This provides your server with additional visibility and helps you attract new members. Additionally, you will gain access to exclusive resources and support from our team.

3. Membership Without Partnership

If you do not wish to apply as a partner, you can simply remain a member of our community. As a member, you can search for servers, engage in discussions, and benefit from the advantages and activities on our Discord server.

4. Contact and Further Information

We look forward to your application and the possibility of welcoming you as a new partner. If you have any questions or need further information, we are always available on our Discord server.
